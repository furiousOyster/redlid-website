COVID-19 UPDATE

Dear Valued Red Lid Garden Bins & Bags Customers, and Red Lid Skip Customers,

Following the Government’s announcement of the Level 4 Alert, Monday 23rd March 2020, effective immediately, we have suspended our services until further notice. Our staff are at home with their families and our office is unattended. At this stage we are unfortunately not able to provide a re-start of collections date. Please store your bin or bag safely on your section but in a place that is accessible. Please note that collections may start again and we may not be able to advise you immediately, hence the reason that your bin or bag is accessible for the driver. This is the information we received from the Government Essential Services Covid-19 Response Team:

Under Alert Level 4, waste (eg "Rubbish Collection") service, under the Utilities and Communications section on the Essential Business list covers essential waste only. GREEN WASTE IS NOT CONSIDERED ESSENTIAL, AS IT DOES NOT ENDANGER PEOPLE IF IT'S NOT COLLECTED. UNFORTUNATELY, AT THIS TIME, IF PEOPLE'S GREEN WASTE IS OVERFLOWING, THEY WILL NEED TO DISPOSE OF IT WITH THE NORMAL RUBBISH.

Please note, all accounts will be on hold with current balances in place. No further charges will be added until our services resume. The Red Lid Team would like to thank all our loyal customers for their continued support. We wish everyone a safe and healthy period of lockdown.

Take care, The Red Lid Team
